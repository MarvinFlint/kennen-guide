Short Gameplay Guide

Lanephase

Most common botlaners will have an edge over you early due to either having superior range or a stronger early game kit. Try only to trade with your empowered auto attack up or throw out the occasional q when you see a clear path.

Teamfights

As your teams adc your job is keeping an eye on your own position and kiting enemy divers. Watch your flanks and don't hesitate to use your ultimate to self-peel. You need to approach teamfights fundamentally different when playing ad, as your abilities will deal next to no damage and only surve a utilitarian purpose.
