Short Gameplay Guide

Lanephase

Your goal in lane is to abuse Klepto as much as possible. If you throw a basic attack and activate your E (or if you are far enough away, Q and W work as well) the basic attack that is mid-air will still apply kleptomancy. Use this to your advanatge and pay attention to always use up both stacks before using another spell (unless you want to deal the maximum amount of damage of course). Don't neglect farming minions and always keep your vision up to avoid falling prey to ganks. Your constant harass will cause the wave to push and expose you to more jungle attention than you'd like.

Teamfights

Your primary objective in teamfights is to engage from a flanking position or to counterenagage when the enenmy team is going in. You can act both as a diver into the enemy backline (especially true with flash) or peel for your own carries with the cc you provide. The later the game goes and the more ap you amass the more devastating your are to squishy enemy carries.
