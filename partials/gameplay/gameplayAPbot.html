Short Gameplay Guide

Lanephase

Your goal in the laningphase depends on the matchup you face. Generally you want to poke / wittle down melee opponents while staying safe from ranged ones. Your autoattacks mean much more early on in the lane than later down the line, so you should use them as much as possible. Your w-empowered autoattack is especially good at zoning enemies off minions or gain a very favorable trade.

Teamfights

Your primary objective in teamfights is to engage from a flanking position or to counterenagage when the enenmy team is going in. You can act both as a diver into the enemy backline (especially true with flash) or peel for your own carries with the cc you provide. The later the game goes and the more ap you amass the more devastating your are to squishy enemy carries. A good flank engage post 25 minutes can easily solowin you a game or secure your team an objective, such as Baron Nashor or an elemental Dragon.
