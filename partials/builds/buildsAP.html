Standard

Your standard AP build. Always go for Protobelt frist and adapt from there. In case neither the magic penetration nor the healing reduction are of any use, replace Morellomonicon with one of the situational items.

Cdr / Utility

This build focusses less on damage and more on utility and availability early by rushing 45% cdr. If you already have multiple carries on your team or you yourself fall behind early, this is a good way to stay relevant with frequent ultimates and flashes.
