Builds

Standard

Your standard AP build. Always go for Protobelt first and adapt from there. In case neither the magic penetration nor the healing reduction are of any use, replace Morellomonicon with one of the situational items.

Cdr / Utility

This build focuses less on damage and more on utility and availability early by rushing 45% cdr. If you already have multiple carries on your team or you yourself fall behind early, this is a good way to stay relevant with frequent ultimates and flashes.

Situational Items

If you start snowballing early, Spellbinder provides you with a great deal of power for a short time at very little relative cost. You need to make sure to use this window however, otherwise Rabadons is a safer and stronger option.

If you find yourself the target of enemy engage or need to cancel out heavy disengage such as Janna ultimate or Lee ultimate consider getting this item earlier. Also if the enemy team has multiple magic damage threats this is a good purchase.

Contrary to popular belief, Zhonyas is a very situational item that you should only get if very specific conditions are met. This means that either Karthus, Kayn or Fizz are on the enemy team or you find yourself unable to survive long enough to get your damage off vs really burst-heavy teams.
