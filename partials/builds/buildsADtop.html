Builds

On-hit

This build focuses on splitpushing and dueling. Frozen mallet provides you a permanent slow to keep melee champions at range while whittling down their health with botrk and rageblade.

Situational Items

Hurricane provides you with additional pushing-power and potentially allows you to slow multiple opponents in combination with Frozen Mallet. Only get this if you really need the waveclear, otherwise it is no worth it.

Should the need arise you can exchange your boots to a defensive option. If the enemy team contains multiple physical damage threats, especially those relying on basic attacks, getting Ninja Tabi is essential. Simply facing one of these in lane can justify them (e.g. Tryn, Jax or Riven).
Same goes for Mercury Treads - opponents relying on their cc to land their spells or simply multiple magic damage threats usually warrant this choice over Berserkers Greaves.
